Travel insurance: simple & flexible



1.1 BWT strongly recommends that Clients have comprehensive travel insurance. Insurance should cover, but not be limited to, cancellation fees and loss of deposit, damage to and loss of baggage and money, medical and hospitalization expenses, repatriation or curtailment expenses due to illness, the need to return home because of an unexpected death or illness in the immediate family, evacuation expenses and accidental death or disability. Clients should obtain exclusion waivers where appropriate for adventure activities excluded in travel insurance policies. The insurance policy numbers and details of the Client, will need be filled in to the on-line Personal Details Form or at the latest be collected by local guide on first arrival to the destination.
It is the Client’s sole responsibility to obtain the appropriate insurance cover as set out in clause 1.1 of these Booking Conditions.
